Biography of Lilian stratford

Lilian has been trading the financial markets
privately on his own account for well over a decade.
As well as his extensive trading experience,
he also holds BSc Economics and Business Finance and
MSc Advanced Financial Economics degrees.
He has operated in a range of markets and has
extensive knowledge of market bubbles, intermarket correlation,
asset pricing and portfolio hedging.
A skilled day trader, Lilian’s approach combines both
fundamental and technical analysis. His focus has shifted
over the years and today he more often favours medium
to long-term positions trading on stocks and intraday
swing trading on European and US markets.
In addition to trading, Lilian has developed this website.
Originally all the information and tools you will find here were developed
for his own use by a dedicated research team.
However Lilian has now made this material available for fellow traders or
just those wishing to learn how to privately trade the financial markets.
